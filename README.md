# PCB-Design-Projects
Hello, these are my PCB lab reports and projects from ECEN 3730 at CU Boulder. Through this class, I learned to how to design PCBs and the process behind manufacturing them. I also learned how to apply and design with best practices and the fundamentals of signal integrity. 

# Board 1:
The objective of this board is to become familiar with the entire design process, from a napkin sktech to an entirely functional PCB. This board will include a 555 timer circuit that operates in astable mode to drive multiple LEDs. The parts will be soldered manually, tested for function, the internal resistance of the 555 output, and the noise on the power rail in the switching of the 555's output.
